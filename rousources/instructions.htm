How To Play Memory Game

You have unlimited attempts to find matches in the panels.

On each turn, a player flips over two cards.

You can use your mouse to pick and choose which tiles you want to match. If the two cards match, they remain face up, and you continue choosing tiles to match. If the two cards do not match, they will be visible to 2 second and then, they are flipped back over face down. Pressing the Esc key will close the Leaderboard dialog and the Memory game.
